# Weather Comfort and Safety Tips

## High Temperature + High Humidity
- Stay hydrated; wear light, breathable clothing.
- Limit strenuous outdoor activity during peak heat.
- Seek shade or air conditioning when the heat index is high.

## Low Humidity
- Use a humidifier indoors in winter to avoid dry skin and throat.
- Apply moisturizer and lip balm more often.

## Cold and Dry (Low Min Temp, Low RH)
- Layer clothing; protect extremities.
- Indoor heating can make RH very low; consider a humidifier.

## General
- Check max/min temperatures to plan clothing and activities.
- Relative humidity helps you decide if it will feel muggy or crisp.
